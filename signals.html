Sonartra Signals

Signals is a structured behavioural assessment designed as a data collection layer for enterprise performance intelligence. It captures how individuals make decisions, lead under pressure, collaborate, and operate in different systems.

What it measures

  • Behavioural style (Driver, Influencer, Stabiliser, Analyst)
  • Core motivators (Achievement, Influence, Stability, Mastery)
  • Leadership orientation (Results, Vision, People, Process)
  • Conflict style and stress behaviour patterns
  • Cultural and cognitive preferences

How outputs are used

  • Role alignment and leadership development
  • Team composition and communication fit
  • Decision-making and execution diagnostics
  • Early behavioural risk signal detection
  • Future performance intelligence modeling
